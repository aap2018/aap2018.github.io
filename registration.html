REGISTRATION POLICY

1) Please note that a separate registration form must be used for each participant.

2) Please be informed that registration is not transferable to another person.

3) Registration will only be valid when payment is received in full by the Malaysian Association for Prosthodontics.

4) Delegates who wish to attend pre-conference workshops MUST register for the main conference EXCEPT for Dental Technologist Workshop

5) All poster presenters must register for the main conference upon acceptance of abstract. Last date for registration for poster presenters will be on 10th August 2018

6) For on-site registration of local and international delegates, ONLY cash will be accepted.

7) The organizing committee reserves the right to change or cancel the programme without prior notice if circumstances dictate.

8) The organizing committee will provide e-certificates to all delegates. It is the responsibility of the delegate to complete their e-survey form to receive the e-certificates.

9) An acknowledgement e-mail will be sent once the payment transactions are completed successfully

10) Participants who register for the conference will be allowed to attend the FORUM on Maxillofacial Rehabilitation and Speech for FREE. Kindly remember to indicate (√) in the registration form if you will attend the forum.

11)Please take note that registration package includes ONLY the following:

    a. Entry for 3 days main conference
    b. Name tag with lanyard
    c. Entrance to lecture halls (except visitors and accompanying persons)
    d. Access to exhibition area
    e. Invitation to opening ceremony

CANCELLATION POLICY
